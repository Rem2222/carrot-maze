# Requirements: Carrot Maze

## R1: Game Field
The game SHALL render a grid-based maze of carrot beds on an HTML5 Canvas.
The maze SHALL have walls (paths between beds) that constrain movement.

## R2: Python Snake
The player SHALL control a snake (python) using arrow keys or WASD.
The snake SHALL grow longer with each rabbit eaten.
The snake SHALL die if it hits a wall or itself.

## R3: Rabbits
Rabbits SHALL spawn on carrot beds and jump between adjacent beds at intervals.
A rabbit eaten by the snake SHALL award 10 points.
At least 3 rabbits SHALL be present on the field at all times.

## R4: User Experience
The game SHALL display current score.
The game SHALL allow restart after death.
The game SHALL be visually appealing with distinct colors for snake, rabbits, carrots, and walls.
